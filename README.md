# startup

The idea is a way to easily compare your own "tier lists" with your friends. Capitalizing on the fun and popular concept of ranking items, it solves the problem of those lists being hard to compare with your friends.
Once you create your list, the site can show you the most interesting comparisons between your list and a friends. For example, which items were collectively ranked highest or lowest, or which items were ranked the most differently.
